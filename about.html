Mission Statement

At MeetingAgent, we believe meetings should drive action, not create more chaos. Our mission is to help teams spend less time managing meetings and more time achieving results through intelligent automation that works quietly in the background.

MeetingAgent's History

MeetingAgent was founded after its creators saw how much time and effort was lost trying to follow up on decisions and action items after meetings. What began as a simple internal tool grew into a full startup idea focused on turning conversations into useful outcomes.
