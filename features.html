Mission Statement

At MeetingAgent, we believe meetings should drive action, not create more chaos. Our mission is to help teams spend less time managing meetings and more time achieving results through intelligent automation that works quietly in the background, so you don't have to.

 

MeetingAgent's History

MeetingAgent was founded by Gurjas Ranu and Sara Chen after they experienced firsthand how much time and effort was lost trying to follow up on meeting action items. What started as a simple internal tool for their own team quickly grew into a full platform used by an wide array of businesses. Today, MeetingAgent continues to evolve, driven by a commitment to making every meeting count.
