Why MeetingAgent?

Stop letting important decisions fall through the cracks. MeetingAgent automatically handles everything after your meeting ends, so your team can focus on what matters.

Core Function and Features

AI Meeting Notes

MeetingAgent joins your Zoom, Teams or Google Meet call and generates clean, structured notes in real time. No more scrambling to write things down.

Action Item Detection

Our AI automatically identifies action items from your meeting conversation and assigns them to the right team members — no manual input required.

Smart Follow-Up Reminders

MeetingAgent sends automated reminders via email or Slack until every action item is marked complete. Nothing gets forgotten.

Team Dashboard

Track your team's meeting activity and task completion rates in one place. See what's done, what's overdue, and who's accountable.

Multi-Format Note Taking

MeetingAgent goes beyond live meetings. Upload any video, audio recording, or text document and our AI will generate structured notes, summaries, and action items perfect for catching up on recorded sessions or analysing written reports.
