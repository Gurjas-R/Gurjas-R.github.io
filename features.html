Why MeetingAgent?

Core functions and features

Stop letting important decisions fall through the cracks. MeetingAgent automatically handles the note-taking work after your meeting ends, so your team can focus on what matters.

Automatic Meeting Notes

MeetingAgent can join online meetings and create structured notes from the conversation without users having to type everything manually.

Document Summaries

Users can upload reports, readings, and other documents to receive concise summaries and organised study notes.

Audio and Video Uploads

The platform turns lecture recordings, interviews, podcasts, and videos into readable notes and key takeaways.

Action Item Extraction

Important tasks, deadlines, and follow-up items are highlighted automatically so users know what needs to happen next.

Clear Summaries

MeetingAgent condenses long discussions into short summaries that are easier to review, share, and act on.

Study and Team Support

The tool is useful for both education and work, helping students revise and helping teams keep track of important decisions.
